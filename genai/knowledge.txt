Low soil moisture requires immediate irrigation to prevent crop stress.

High temperature increases evaporation, requiring more frequent irrigation.

Excessive irrigation can damage roots and reduce oxygen in soil.

Balanced NPK improves plant resilience and reduces water dependency.

Poor plant health combined with dry soil indicates urgent watering is needed.

High humidity reduces evaporation, so irrigation should be reduced.
